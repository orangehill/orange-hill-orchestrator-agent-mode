# Orange Hill — AI Delivery Orchestrator · Agent Mode

This repo is the companion to our open **AI Delivery Orchestrator** role. It's built to be read by **your** AI. Point your agent at it and it will explain the role, judge your fit against what it knows about you, run the application task with you, and help you apply — honestly, including if this isn't your role.

> We've largely automated how we build. A static PDF job ad would be off-brand. So: talk to the job.

## Connect your agent

Paste this into ChatGPT, Claude, Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, or any agent you use:

```text
You're helping me decide whether to apply for the AI Delivery Orchestrator role at
Orange Hill, an AI-native software studio (Serbia + DACH). Be my advisor, not their
recruiter — be honest, including if I'm not a fit.

Use this public repo as your source of truth — clone it, or fetch the raw files:
https://github.com/orangehill/orange-hill-orchestrator-agent-mode

Read first: AGENTS.md, role.md, company/orange-hill-dna.md, and
apply/application-task.md (it links apply/the-week.md — the actual task).

Then:
1. Give me the cleanest 5-bullet version of the role and how Orange Hill works.
2. Using what you already know about me — my background, projects, and how I work —
   tell me honestly how I'd fit, where I'd shine, and where I'd struggle. If you
   don't have enough context, ask me up to 5 questions first.
3. Offer to: (a) walk me through the application task, (b) prep me for the
   interview, or (c) draft a tailored application I can send.

Don't sell me. Help me make a good decision.
```

Using a fresh chat that doesn't know you yet? **Give your agent your CV or LinkedIn first** — it can only judge your fit against what it actually knows about you.

## Why a job post you can talk to
Because your AI can know you better than any bullet list, and we'd rather it tell you the truth about fit than have you guess. This repo is open — fork it for your own hiring.
