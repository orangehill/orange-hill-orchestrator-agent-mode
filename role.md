# AI Delivery Orchestrator

**We don't write much code anymore — we orchestrate the AI that does. We're hiring one more to build the same way.**

Orange Hill is a small, senior, profitable software studio that builds and maintains platforms for high-profile clients — the Serbian Chamber of Commerce ([pks.rs](https://pks.rs)), m:tel Austria ([mtel.at](https://mtel.at)), Eucerin, Coca-Cola, and others around the world — across Serbia and the DACH region. **The majority of our delivery already runs on AI agents** — we're at the forefront of AI-native development, and we intend to stay there. We're not hiring someone to type faster. We're hiring someone to **direct**.

## What you'll own
- Run several client projects **in parallel** — not by coding each by hand, but by orchestrating AI agents and steering them to a high-quality result.
- Own delivery end to end: architecture decisions, the path to production (DevOps, servers, integrations), and the quality bar.
- Be the **taste in the room** — judge what's good, what's well-planned, what's genuinely good UX, and kill what isn't.
- Turn vague client goals into shippable products, fast.

## Who you are
- **Ruthlessly organized.** You can hold several projects in your head and keep every one moving. This is the trait we care about most — it's what makes parallelism and agent orchestration possible.
- **A real engineer.** You've built and shipped software, you understand servers and DevOps, and you know your way around third-party APIs and services. You can judge code even when you're not the one writing it.
- **Creative, with taste.** You believe the best builders are part scientist, part artist. You have opinions about design and UX — you don't have to *be* a designer, but you know good from bad and you care.
- **AI-native by default.** Your instinct is "what's the best way to point AI at this," not "let me hand-code it." You're genuinely excited about where this is going.

## This might not be your role if…
- You love grinding one hard problem in deep solo focus and don't want to let go of the keyboard. (Great engineer — wrong seat.)
- You treat AI tools as a threat or a toy.
- You're brilliant but disorganized. Organization is the non-negotiable here; we can't coach it fast enough.
- You want a narrow IC lane with someone else owning the messy whole.

## How we work
We expect you to **recognize what's good and delegate execution to AI** — not to program the traditional way. You'll run several projects at once, but you know **judgment doesn't parallelize** — so you protect it. If that sounds like a downgrade, this isn't your role. If it sounds like leverage, read [`company/how-we-work.md`](company/how-we-work.md) and [`company/orange-hill-dna.md`](company/orange-hill-dna.md).

## What we offer
- **Real autonomy, ownership, and upside** — high-leverage work with a senior team behind you (base + performance).
- A front-row seat building our **own** AI-first products, not just client work.
- A team that's small, senior, and allergic to bureaucracy.
- A path that grows with our DACH/Austria expansion.
