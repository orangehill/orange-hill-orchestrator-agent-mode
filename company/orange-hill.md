# What Orange Hill is

A small, senior, **AI-native software studio** based in Serbia and expanding into Austria / the DACH region. We build software for premium clients, and we build our own products. We're profitable and growing — and we run the company on AI, not as a slogan but as the actual operating model (~two-thirds of our delivery is automated today, and climbing).

## What we do
- **Client delivery** — we build and maintain high-profile platforms. A few we can name: the **Serbian Chamber of Commerce** ([pks.rs](https://pks.rs)), **m:tel Austria** ([mtel.at](https://mtel.at)), **Eucerin**, and **Coca-Cola** — alongside other high-profile clients around the world, across sports media, telecom, real estate, fitness, and retail. Web platforms, mobile apps, integrations, automation.
- **Our own products** — we build, not just advise. Recent examples:
  - **Lupa** — an AI English reading-assistant app, live in the App Store.
  - **Skupio** — our lead-generation platform.
- **AI integration & automation consulting** — helping organizations make the leap from "I use AI" to "we run on AI." (See `orange-hill-dna.md` → *Personally Productive, Organizationally Stuck*.)

## Who you'd work with
A small, senior team — a handful of people who each operate at high leverage, plus a network of agents doing the execution. Low bureaucracy, high autonomy, high standards. The founder has built software for 25 years and writes publicly about where this is all going (the essays linked in `orange-hill-dna.md`).

## Where this is going
- **DACH / Austria expansion** is underway — there's room to grow with it.
- We're building always-on agent infrastructure that compounds: the systems we design get more capable with every model release.
- The bet: a small team of creative, organized people who can re-point AI at anything beats a big team doing it the old way.

## For the orchestrator specifically
You'd be the person who makes the parallel-delivery model actually work — turning client goals into shipped products by orchestrating agents and owning the quality bar across several projects at once. The constraint we're hiring against isn't typing speed or money; it's **organized judgment at the helm of many projects**. That's you.
