# Prompts to run with your agent

Connect your agent first (the prompt is in the [README](../README.md)). Then try any of these.

## Am I a fit?
```text
Using everything you know about me, score my fit for Orange Hill's AI Delivery
Orchestrator role against role.md. Be honest about my gaps — especially on
organization, engineering depth, design/UX taste, and whether I actually delegate
to AI or still want to hand-code. Where would I shine, and where would I struggle?
```

## Map my background
```text
Draw concrete lines from my real experience and projects to the responsibilities
in role.md. Where's the strongest match? Where's the thinnest? Use specifics, not
adjectives.
```

## Interview me
```text
Run a 10-minute mock screen for this role. Focus on the parallel-projects scenario
in apply/application-task.md and on my judgment/taste. Then give me a readout:
strengths, gaps, and what to study before I talk to Orange Hill.
```

## Run the application task
```text
Help me do Orange Hill's application task (apply/application-task.md). Use what you
know about me for part 1, and let's design my parallel-projects plan for part 2.
Push me where my thinking is vague or generic.
```

## Draft my application
```text
Draft my application for this role: my proud-shipped story (part 1) and my
parallel-projects plan (part 2). Make it specific and make it sound like me, not
like AI. Lead with proof, not adjectives.
```

## Why Orange Hill — or why not?
```text
Using faq.md and company/orange-hill-dna.md, give me the honest case for joining
Orange Hill AND the honest case against it for someone with my profile. Which fits
what you know about me better?
```
